About the lot

A spot you can count on, all year long

Main Street Parking has served Massachusetts Maritime Academy cadets for years. Our lots sit just behind 111 and 121 Main Street in Buzzards Bay — a quick six-tenths of a mile from the Academy entrance, so you can walk, bike, or drive over in minutes.

Whether you're an incoming freshman figuring out where to leave your car or a returning cadet renewing your spot, the process is simple: reserve online, send payment by Venmo or check, and your decal arrives in the mail.

Good to know

Lot rules

A few simple things that keep the lot safe and running smoothly for every cadet.

01

Decal required

Every vehicle must display a valid decal. Vehicles without one will be towed at the owner's expense.

02

Full-year passes only

No short-term passes and no Spring 2027–only passes are available. Spaces are limited.

03

Keep it clean

Don't throw trash from your vehicle. Littering means your pass is revoked with no refund.

04

Park at your own risk

The lot is monitored daily, but the owner isn't responsible for any damage to vehicles.

05

Limited spaces

Space is tighter this year due to new neighboring businesses, so reserve early.

06

Be a good neighbor

You are who you are when no one is looking. Respect the lot and the surrounding businesses.
